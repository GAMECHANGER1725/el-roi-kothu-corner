Our Story

Western Sydney's
Hidden Gem.

El Roi Kothu Corner is a Sri Lankan and Indian-inspired street food truck rolling through Western Sydney. We're best known for our kothu roti and dosa — bold, authentic flavours cooked fresh to order on the hot plate.

Based out of Pendle Hill and trading across Blacktown and nearby suburbs, we're the kind of place Sri Lankan food fans call their late-night go-to. More of a community hangout than a sit-down restaurant — real street food, real people, real flavour.

From the Hot Plate

Signature Dishes

Kothu roti, dosa and more — all cooked to order, every time. No shortcuts, no reheated plates.

Kothu Roti
Shredded roti tossed on the iron griddle with egg, vegetables and your choice of chicken, mutton, or prawn — the signature dish.
Signature
Dosa
Crispy fermented rice crepe cooked to a golden crunch, served with sambar and fresh coconut chutney.
Fan Favourite
Mutton Kothu
The bold, slow-cooked mutton version of our kothu — rich spice, generous portions, built for serious flavour cravings.
Weekend Special
Masala Dosa
Crispy dosa stuffed with spiced potato masala — a South Indian classic done the way it should be.
Vegetarian
Egg Kothu
Classic egg kothu — scrambled through shredded roti with fresh aromatics and a house spice blend. Late-night comfort in a plate.
Classic
Prawn Kothu
Juicy prawns folded through hot-plate kothu with spiced gravy and fresh coriander — a seafood lover's pick.
